Model releases & capability updates

Anthropic
Claude Sonnet 5 launches as the new default model [source]
Anthropic released Claude Sonnet 5, its most agentic Sonnet model yet, with clear gains over Sonnet 4.6 in coding, agentic search, tool use, and multimodal reasoning. It's the default model for Free and Pro plans and is also available to Max, Team, and Enterprise users, with introductory pricing of $2/$10 per million input/output tokens through August 31, 2026.
Why it matters: Sonnet 5 is also GA in Microsoft Foundry on Azure alongside Opus 4.8 and Haiku 4.5, so D365/Power Platform partners already calling Claude via Azure AI Foundry get an immediate capability and pricing upgrade with no migration work.
Google
Nano Banana 2 Lite and Gemini Omni Flash ship in public preview [source]
Google released Nano Banana 2 Lite, the fastest and most cost-efficient model in its Nano Banana image family, alongside Gemini Omni Flash for video generation and conversational editing. Both are live in Google AI Studio, the Gemini API, and the Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform, and are rolling out to consumer surfaces like AI Mode in Search.
Why it matters: Cheaper, faster multimodal generation lowers the cost of adding Gemini-powered image/video features to any Copilot Studio or Power Platform integration that calls the Gemini API.

Microsoft
Copilot-inclusive Microsoft 365 Business SKUs become permanent [source]
As of July 1, 2026, Microsoft 365 Business Standard with Copilot ($23.50/user/mo) and Business Premium with Copilot ($32/user/mo) are permanent, generally available SKUs rather than a time-limited promotional bundle.
Why it matters: Removes the friction of quoting Copilot as a separate add-on for SMB customers — partners can now sell a single, standard bundled seat alongside D365 deployments.
Anthropic
US lifts export controls on Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 [source]
The Commerce Department lifted its export-control directive that had forced Anthropic to suspend foreign-national access to Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 after a reported safety bypass. Anthropic is redeploying Fable 5 starting July 1 with updated cybersecurity safeguards, restoring global access on the Claude platform, Claude.ai, Claude Code, and Claude Cowork, with re-enablement on AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Foundry to follow.
Why it matters: Partners with global teams that lost Fable 5 access get their most capable Claude model back on Azure/Microsoft Foundry — and it's a reminder that frontier-model access can be pulled or restored on short notice for national-security reasons.

OpenAI
GeneBench-Pro shows top models still fail most real biology reasoning [source]
OpenAI introduced GeneBench-Pro, a 129-problem benchmark testing AI judgment on realistic, ambiguous computational-biology research questions rather than factual recall, validated against external researchers. Its strongest model, GPT-5.6 Sol, solved only 28.7-31.5% of problems; Anthropic's Claude Opus 4.8 was the best non-OpenAI model at 16.0%.
Why it matters: A useful reality check for any life-sciences or R&D copilot work: even frontier models remain far from reliable on open-ended scientific judgment tasks.
