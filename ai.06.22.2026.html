Samsung Electronics deploys ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex across global workforce [source]
OpenAI announced June 21 that Samsung Electronics is rolling out ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex to all employees in Korea and all Device eXperience (DX) division employees worldwide — one of OpenAI's largest enterprise deployments to date. The rollout reverses a company-wide ChatGPT ban Samsung imposed in 2023 after an internal source-code leak, with the semiconductor Device Solutions unit remaining under tighter restrictions. Non-technical employees can use Codex to turn plain-language descriptions into working software, internal tools, websites, and automated workflows.
Why it matters: A major enterprise AI validation from a global technology manufacturer, signaling that OpenAI's data-protection guarantees are clearing corporate security bars. Relevant to Microsoft Partners advising clients on AI tool governance and ChatGPT-vs-Copilot for M365 positioning.
