Notable research or safety news

Google (+ Microsoft, GitHub, others)
Agentic Resource Discovery (ARD) specification published. [source]
ARD is an open spec (Apache 2.0, built on the Linux Foundation AI Catalog data model) for publishing, discovering, and verifying AI tool/agent capabilities across federated registries, so agents can find capabilities at runtime instead of pre-installing them. Eleven companies — including Google, Microsoft, GitHub, Hugging Face, NVIDIA, Salesforce, ServiceNow, and Snowflake — backed it.
Why it matters: Highly relevant to agent-builders. Microsoft is a co-publisher, so ARD will likely intersect with Copilot Studio / Foundry agent tooling and MCP — a standardized discovery layer affects how you architect multi-agent, tool-connected solutions.
